MUSIC CONCEPT APP


Role: UX UI Designer, Researcher
Duration: 3 days
Software: Figma

This music social networking app I designed was a concept I came up with as a quick exercise. Essentially this app functions as a hub where creatives from the art world can come together to collaborate on audio/visual projects with one another.

While it mainly functions as an app for musicians to collaborate, it also includes videographers, photographers, artists, designers, and many other kinds of creatives.





BACKGROUND

Context and Background


As a musician myself, I found it difficult to collaborate with other artists through conventional social media platforms such as Twitter and Instagram. One of the reasons being that it was difficult to get in contact with artists who have larger followings. Itâ€™s all too easy to be drowned out by fans or other parties who are bombarding their notifications. The other reason being that it would be really convenient if there was a hub that could make it easier for artists to find and collaborate with each other.

With this pain point in mind, I decided to craft an experience for artists and creatives where they can come together and directly contact each other without the distractions that other social media platforms offer. This platform would be art specific.

In this app, users would also have the ability to post audio files, photos, videos, and text posts. I also designed an explore page where users can easily discover new artists by category.

I wanted to go for a cool, but also familiar visual style and design that users can experience while navigating the app.
