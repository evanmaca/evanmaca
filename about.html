WHO AM I?


Hello, my name is Evan Macadangdang! I’m a UX/UI designer who majored in New Media Design at Cal State University Fresno.

I graduated with my Bachelors degree in May of 2022. I’m currently based in Fresno, California.

I enjoy designing experiences where people can feel comfortable, and confident. Art has always been a big part of my journey as a designer. As a child I loved to draw and sketch. Specifically I enjoyed drawing portraits of people, movie characters, and comics. This eventually lead to my interest in design, ultimately leading to my passion for UX/UI design.

Because I also have a passion for fashion, video games, music, and film, those mediums end up subconsciously inspiring me in unique ways in my design process.
