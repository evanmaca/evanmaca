IKEA VIRTUAL SHOWROOM


Role: UX UI Designer, Researcher
Duration: 3 Weeks
Software: Figma, Adobe Illustrator

I wanted to simulate an IKEA showroom as a continuation of my IKEA website redesign case study. To do so, I came up with an idea to implement a 360° photo view.

DESIGN

Features


The 360° photo view gives online users an immersive IKEA showroom experience.

Online users can see styled IKEA furniture next to each other in a home setting and use a measurement tool to get precise distances between products.

This caters to product aware shoppers who want more details on IKEA products (measurements, view from all angles, etc).

This caters to product unaware shoppers to give them inspiration and the ability to see furniture and decor paired together in a virtually simulated space.

How to use

Users can use their mouse and WASD keys on their keyboard to navigate and explore the virtual showroom





Product Browsing

By entering "Product Mode", the user is able to click on various products styled across the showroom and view its details





Measurement

By entering "Measurement Mode", the user is able to measure and record the sizes of the ikea products on display
